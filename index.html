What Is An Interior Design Concept?

Interior design concepts are the big-picture building blocks of any room’s design.
These concepts are used as guides to make important design decisions such as what type of high-end flooring to use,
what artisan piece of luxury furniture will make the space pop, and which dreamy drapery is best for a room.
Think of interior design concepts as elements that work together to create a cohesive and beautiful room.
As your interior designer, we will use these concepts through the conceptual design phase to develop your vision and bring it to life.
